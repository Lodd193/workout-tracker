## üí° Training Tips

### Progressive Overload
- Gradually increase weight, reps, or sets over time
- Track your workouts to monitor progress
- Aim for 1-2 rep improvement per week on key lifts

### Exercise Selection
- Choose 4-6 exercises per workout
- Include both compound and isolation movements
- Vary exercises every 4-8 weeks to prevent plateaus

### Volume Guidelines
- **Chest**: 10-20 sets per week
- **Back**: 12-24 sets per week
- **Shoulders**: 8-16 sets per week
- **Arms**: 6-12 sets per muscle group per week
- **Legs**: 12-20 sets per week
- **Core**: 6-12 sets per week

### Rest Periods
- **Compound lifts (squat, bench, row)**: 2-3 minutes
- **Isolation exercises**: 60-90 seconds
- **Cardio intervals**: 1-2 minutes
